## Company-Wide Lists
all-company@servicedesk-simulator.com - All employees (except guests)

sales-team@servicedesk-simulator.com - Sales department

engineering-team@servicedesk-simulator.com - Engineering/IT department

finance-team@servicedesk-simulator.com - Finance and Accounting

marketing-team@servicedesk-simulator.com - Marketing department

operations-team@servicedesk-simulator.com - Operations staff

## Requesting List Changes
Submit ticket to helpdesk with:
• List name (e.g., sales-team@servicedesk-simulator.com)
• Change requested (add/remove member)
• Employee name and email
• Requestor name and manager approval
Processing time:
1 business day
List owner:
IT Systems team (can be overridden by manager)

## Mobile/Mail Access
Lists appear in Global Address Book (GAB)

Search by list name in To:
field
Members listed when composing - verify before sending to large group
